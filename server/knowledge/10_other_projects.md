# Other Projects

## MDVRP Solver — Beltone AI Hackathon
Multi-Depot Vehicle Routing Problem solver that hit 78–88% order fulfillment on the competition test set. Implemented BFS pathfinding over the road-network graph, multi-order packing with capacity-aware route construction, and three-level fallback algorithms when the primary solver couldn't fit an order. Shipped a Streamlit dashboard for route visualization and live testing. Team name: Vibe Coders.
